Building Indonesia's Future, One Project at a Time

PT Prakarsa stands as one of Indonesia's premier construction and civil engineering companies, with over two decades of experience in delivering exceptional infrastructure solutions across the archipelago.

What began as CERTC (Civil Engineering Research and Technology Center) has evolved into Prakarsa, a company that embodies innovation, reliability, and sustainable development practices. Our transformation reflects our commitment to growing alongside Indonesia's dynamic infrastructure needs.

Today, we specialize in large-scale construction projects, from residential complexes and commercial buildings to critical infrastructure that connects communities and drives economic growth.
